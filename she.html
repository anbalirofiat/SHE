SHE Soars

SHE Soars Program

The CFK SHE Soars Program makes post secondary education possible for girls from lower-income families.

It is true that once you become a SHE Girl, you become a part of the CKF family. CKF has been a family to me in every way.

SPONSORSHIP DETAILS

Value

The scholarship value is pegged at N300,000/annum and is estimated to cover tuition, books, and a modest monthly living stipend.

Duration

The sponsorship duration would span the student’s entire course of study, which in our estimate would be 4-6 years depending on the course.

Eligibility

Applicants must be female graduates of a Nigerian public secondary school and have proof of admission into a Nigerian public tertiary institution.

Application

Applications for the 2024/2025 session is now open and closes on Thursday, November 13, 2025 by 5:00PM.
